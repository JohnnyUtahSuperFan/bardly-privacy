Bardly Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

What data does Bardly collect?

Bardly stores your writing entries locally on your device. This includes the text you write and the date/time of each entry.

Is my data shared?

No. Your writings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any server. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data.

Notifications

Bardly uses local notifications to remind you to write. These are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Data deletion

You can delete any writing entry within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.
